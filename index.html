Enhancing Cultural Heritage Knowledge

Madonna con Bambino, San Giovannino e angeli — Sandro Botticelli in ArCo

KE4H project — evidence-based knowledge gap identification and RDF enrichment

Abstract

The purpose of this project is to present the complete methodology used to identify, validate and enrich semantic knowledge gaps in the ArCo Knowledge Graph. Every proposed enrichment is supported by SPARQL exploration, comparison with the existing RDF description, validation through multiple Large Language Models and transformation into RDF triples.

The selected case study is Sandro Botticelli's Madonna con Bambino, San Giovannino e angeli (HistoricOrArtisticProperty/1201008107). SPARQL queries confirmed rich catalogue metadata but missing iconographic, symbolic, theological and Renaissance contextual semantics. ChatGPT and Gemini were compared using Standard, Few-shot and Chain-of-Thought prompting. Validated concepts were transformed into RDF triples for illustrative knowledge graph enrichment.
