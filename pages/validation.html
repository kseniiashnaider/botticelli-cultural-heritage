Validation

12. Validation Strategy

Every candidate concept extracted from the LLMs was verified before inclusion in the enrichment model. Concepts already represented in ArCo were excluded. New concepts were accepted only if they were consistently identified by multiple language models and agreed with established art-historical literature. This conservative validation strategy reduced hallucination risk and ensured that the proposed RDF triples remained semantically meaningful.
