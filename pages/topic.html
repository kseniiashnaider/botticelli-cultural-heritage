Sandro Botticelli in the ArCo Knowledge Graph

Topic

Sandro Botticelli (born Alessandro di Mariano Filipepi) was one of the leading painters of the Italian Renaissance. Works such as The Birth of Venus and Primavera are frequently discussed in relation to Neoplatonic and humanist culture in Florence.

Why ArCo?

The ArCo (Architettura del Conoscimento) knowledge graph catalogues Italian cultural heritage using a network of ontologies. It is well suited to this project because it combines catalogue metadata, context description and links to photographic documentation — allowing us to compare what is explicitly modelled in RDF with what appears only in related resource labels.
